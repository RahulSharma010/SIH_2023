What we do ?

We are excited to present our innovative solution for the Smart India Hackathon, centered around the theme of clean and green technology and smart waste management systems. Our proposal focuses on the implementation of Smart Waste Bins (SWBs) to address the growing environmental and sanitation challenges in India.

Our SWBs are equipped with state-of-the-art sensor technology, enabling real-time monitoring of waste levels, temperature, and humidity. They are also equipped with compaction systems to maximize bin capacity, reducing the frequency of collection trips. Additionally, the SWBs feature user-friendly interfaces, allowing citizens to interact with them via mobile apps, helping them locate nearby bins and providing insights on proper waste disposal.

Furthermore, our system incorporates machine learning algorithms to predict waste generation patterns, optimizing waste collection routes for municipal authorities. By streamlining the waste management process, we not only reduce operational costs but also significantly decrease carbon emissions associated with collection vehicles.
